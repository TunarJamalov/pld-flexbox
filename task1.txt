●  Write a brief explanation of Flexbox and its purpose in modern web design. 
Answer:
Flexbox is a CSS layout module designed to arrange elements efficiently within a container, even when their sizes are dynamic.
Flexbox makes it easier to build responsive, flexible, and adaptive layouts without relying on floats or complex hacks


●  Why is Flexbox considered better than traditional float positioning or inline-block 
layouts?
Answer: 
Auto handles alignment and spacing
Easier vertical and horizontal centering
Cleaner, more readable code
