Task 5: Flexbox Shorthands 
Question: What are the shorthand properties for flex in CSS? 
●  Explain how the flex shorthand property works and what values it can take. 

Answer:
Shorthands properties are: flex-grow, flex-shrink and flex-basis

1.flex-grow (number) — how much the item will grow relative to others (default: 0).

2.flex-shrink (number) — how much the item will shrink if needed (default: 1).

3.flex-basis (length or keyword) — the initial size of the item before growing or shrinking (default: auto).
